Kingston's favourite waterpark offers a 10-metre water slide, a lazy river, toddler pool, family and accessible change rooms, shaded areas and zero-beach entry.

Facility Features

- 25 metre lane pool  
- Leisure pool  
- Diving board  
- Tot pool  
- Spray features  
- Water slide  
- Lazy River  

### Hours of Operation
Closed for the season
